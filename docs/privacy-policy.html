LinkTiles Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 25, 2025

Overview

LinkTiles is a Chrome extension that helps you organize bookmarks into spaces and projects. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how LinkTiles handles your data.

Data Collection

LinkTiles does not collect, transmit, or store any personal data on external servers.

All your data (spaces, projects, tiles, and settings) is stored locally on your device using your browser's built-in storage (IndexedDB). Your data never leaves your browser.

What LinkTiles Accesses

  • Bookmarks: With your permission, LinkTiles can read your Chrome bookmarks to import them into the extension. This data stays on your device.
  • Tabs: LinkTiles accesses tab information to save the current page and display favicons. This information is not transmitted anywhere.
  • Local Storage: LinkTiles stores your organized bookmarks locally on your device.

External Requests

LinkTiles makes requests to fetch favicon images for your bookmarked sites. These requests are made to:

  • The bookmarked websites themselves (to fetch their favicon)
  • Google's favicon service (google.com/s2/favicons)
  • icon.horse favicon service

These are simple image requests to display icons. No personal data or browsing information is sent with these requests.

Analytics & Tracking

LinkTiles does not include any analytics, telemetry, or tracking. We do not collect usage statistics or any other information about how you use the extension.

Data Sharing

We do not share any data with third parties because we do not collect any data.

Data Export

You can export your LinkTiles data at any time using the built-in export feature. The exported file is saved to your device and is not transmitted anywhere.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please open an issue on our GitHub repository.
